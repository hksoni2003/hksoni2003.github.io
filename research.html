Research Overview

Our research integrates experimental cancer biology, computational genomics, and artificial intelligence to understand molecular mechanisms of disease and identify therapeutic targets.

Research Areas

Cancer Genomics

Multi-omics data integration and gene expression analysis to identify biomarkers and regulatory pathways in cancer.

Cytogenetics

Chromosomal instability and genomic structural alterations associated with cancer and environmental exposures.

Computational Drug Discovery

Structure-based drug discovery approaches including molecular docking and molecular dynamics simulations.

Artificial Intelligence in Biology

Machine learning approaches to analyze genomic, epigenomic and biomedical datasets.
