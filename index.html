Principal Investigator

Dr. Harshit Kumar Soni

Assistant Professor Department of Zoology Government PG College Satna

ORCID: 0000-0002-6165-9154

Dr. Harshit Kumar Soni is a cancer cell biologist and bioinformatics researcher whose work focuses on computational oncology, cytogenetics, and AI-driven biomedical discovery. His research integrates multi-omics analysis, structural biology, and machine learning to understand molecular mechanisms of cancer and identify novel therapeutic targets.

Research Areas

Cancer Genomics

Multi-omics analysis for biomarker discovery and cancer subtype characterization.

Cytogenetics

Chromosomal instability and genomic alterations in cancer development.

Computational Drug Discovery

Structure-based discovery of therapeutic targets using docking and molecular dynamics.

AI in Cancer Biology

Machine learning models for understanding disease mechanisms and predicting biomarkers.

Featured Research Projects

KRAS Cryptic Regulatory Interfaces

AI-driven discovery of cryptic regulatory interfaces in oncogenic KRAS signaling.

Natural GLP-1R Modulator Discovery

Computational discovery of natural compounds modulating the GLP-1 receptor.

Environmental Epigenomics

Environmental-epigenomic network rewiring and cancer susceptibility.

Endocrine Disruptors & Cancer Risk

Cytogenetic and epigenetic effects of environmental toxins in rural populations.
