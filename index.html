Principal Investigator

Dr. Harshit Kumar Soni

Assistant Professor Department of Zoology Government PG College Satna, India

Dr. Harshit Kumar Soni is a cancer cell biologist and bioinformatics researcher working at the intersection of computational biology, genomics, and artificial intelligence. His research focuses on discovering molecular mechanisms and therapeutic targets in cancer using multi-omics analysis and computational modeling.

Research Focus

Cancer Genomics

Integrative analysis of gene expression and multi-omics data to identify biomarkers and therapeutic targets in cancer.

Cytogenetics

Study of chromosomal abnormalities and genomic instability associated with cancer progression.

Computational Drug Discovery

Molecular docking and simulation approaches for identifying novel therapeutic targets and small-molecule modulators.

AI in Biomedical Research

Application of artificial intelligence and machine learning to understand disease mechanisms and predict biomarkers.

Current Research Projects

KRAS Regulatory Interface Discovery

AI-driven discovery of cryptic regulatory interfaces in oncogenic KRAS signaling.

Natural GLP-1R Modulators

Computational screening of natural compounds for GLP-1 receptor modulation.

Environmental Epigenomics

Understanding environmental influences on cancer susceptibility via epigenomic networks.

Endocrine Disruptors and Cancer Risk

Cytogenetic and epigenetic investigation of environmental exposures in rural populations.
